Workplace Announcement

Алдымен аудионы тыңдаңыз. Кейін төмендегі карточкалардан Test, Transcript, Translation және Vocabulary бөлімдерін ашыңыз. Сначала прослушайте аудио. Затем откройте карточки Test, Transcript, Translation и Vocabulary ниже.

Listening · Questions

1. What is the main purpose of the announcement?

C дұрыс. Announcement бірнеше workplace updates береді: management change, car park және canteen payment. C правильно. Announcement даёт несколько workplace updates: management change, car park и canteen payment.

2. When is James Watson leaving his position?

B дұрыс. James Watson is leaving his position at the end of this week. B правильно. James Watson уходит с должности at the end of this week.

3. When will James Watson’s replacement start?

E дұрыс. Replacement is starting at the end of the next month. E правильно. Замена начнёт работу at the end of the next month.

4. What will happen to the staff car park next month?

A дұрыс. Staff car park next month for improvements closed болады. A правильно. Staff car park будет закрыт на улучшения.

5. Where should staff park if they arrive before 8.30 a.m.?

D дұрыс. Before 8.30 a.m. келгендер Brown Street-тегі small car park қолданады. D правильно. До 8:30 нужно использовать small car park on Brown Street.

6. Where should staff go if they arrive after 8.30 a.m.?

B дұрыс. After 8.30 a.m. келгендер directly to the church car park баруы керек. B правильно. После 8:30 нужно ехать directly to the church car park.

7. What rule is mentioned about the church car park?

C дұрыс. Church car park evenings керек болғандықтан, staff before 6 p.m. leave етуі керек. C правильно. Church car park нужен вечером, поэтому нужно leave before 6 p.m.

8. How can staff pay in the canteen now?

A дұрыс. Canteen cashless system енгізді: smartphone немесе company ID card арқылы төлеуге болады. A правильно. В canteen теперь cashless system: smartphone или company ID card.

Transcript

His replacement is starting at the end of the next month.

In the meantime, we'll continue with our projects as usual.

I have two more quick points. Firstly, there will be some improvements made to the staff car park next month for a few weeks.

It will be closed during that time.

We can use the local church car park until our own one is ready.

If you arrive before 8.30 a.m., please use our small car park on Brown Street, and if you arrive after that, you should go directly to the church car park.

It's only a five-minute walk away.

But they need it in the evenings, so you have to leave before 6 p.m.

The canteen has now introduced a cashless payment system.

So, you can't use cash for payments any more.

You can pay directly with your smartphone or you can pay using your company ID card.

The total amount put on your company ID card comes off your salary at the end of each month.

Mixed Grammar Test

Grammar · Questions

1. I usually ____ coffee in the morning, but today I am drinking tea.

C дұрыс. Usually — Present Simple белгісі.C правильно. Usually показывает Present Simple.

2. She ____ in this company since 2020.

B дұрыс. Since 2020 — Present Perfect.B правильно. Since 2020 требует Present Perfect.

3. We arrived late because the train ____ before we reached the station.

E дұрыс. Past action басқа past action-нан бұрын болды: Past Perfect.E правильно. Действие произошло раньше другого past action: Past Perfect.

4. I bought ____ umbrella because it was raining heavily.

A дұрыс. Umbrella дауысты дыбыстан басталады: an.A правильно. Umbrella начинается с гласного звука: an.

5. The book is different ____ the film.

D дұрыс. Correct collocation: different from.D правильно. Правильное сочетание: different from.

6. You ____ wear a seat belt when you drive. It is required by law.

B дұрыс. Required by law — міндет: must.B правильно. Required by law означает обязанность: must.

7. If I had more time, I ____ a new language.

C дұрыс. Second Conditional: If + Past Simple, would + verb.C правильно. Second Conditional: If + Past Simple, would + verb.

8. The report ____ by the manager yesterday.

E дұрыс. Passive Voice, Past Simple: was written.E правильно. Passive Voice, Past Simple: was written.

9. He said that he ____ tired.

A дұрыс. Reported speech: “I am tired” → he said he was tired.A правильно. Reported speech: “I am tired” → he said he was tired.

10. The woman ____ lives next door is a doctor.

B дұрыс. Адам туралы relative pronoun: who.B правильно. Для человека используем relative pronoun: who.

11. She is interested in ____ abroad.

D дұрыс. Interested in + gerund: studying.D правильно. Interested in + gerund: studying.

12. This test is ____ than the previous one.

C дұрыс. Than бар: comparative form — more difficult.C правильно. Than требует comparative form — more difficult.

13. I was tired, ____ I continued working.

E дұрыс. Екі қарама-қарсы ой: tired, but continued.E правильно. Две противоположные идеи: tired, but continued.

14. There isn’t ____ milk left in the fridge.

A дұрыс. Milk — uncountable noun, сондықтан much.A правильно. Milk — неисчисляемое существительное, поэтому much.

15. I look forward to ____ from you soon.

B дұрыс. Look forward to + gerund: hearing.B правильно. Look forward to + gerund: hearing.
Part 3 · Reading

Language and Cultural Identity

Алдымен мәтінді оқыңыз, кейін төмендегі 8 сұраққа жауап беріңіз.Сначала прочитайте текст, затем ответьте на 8 вопросов ниже.

Language is more than a tool for communication. It also carries history, values and cultural memory. Through language, people learn traditional expressions, family stories, songs, jokes and ways of showing respect. For this reason, language is closely connected with cultural identity.

In many countries, people speak more than one language. This can be a strength because it allows them to communicate with different communities and take part in global education, business and culture. At the same time, local and national languages need support. If a language becomes less common, some cultural knowledge may become harder to pass to the next generation.

Schools, families and media can help preserve language. Children learn not only grammar and vocabulary, but also how language is used in real life. Traditional songs, poems, films and books can make a language feel alive and meaningful.

However, language preservation should not mean rejecting other languages. In the modern world, people often need international languages for study and work. The challenge is to find balance: learning global languages while continuing to respect and use one’s own language.

B дұрыс. Мәтінде language history, values және cultural memory тасымалдайды деп берілген.B правильно. The text says language carries history, values and cultural memory.

3. Why can speaking more than one language be a strength?

E дұрыс. More than one language білу different communities-пен communication жасауға көмектеседі.E правильно. Speaking more than one language helps people communicate with different communities.

4. What may happen if a language becomes less common?

A дұрыс. Language less common болса, cultural knowledge next generation-ға жеткізу қиындайды.A правильно. If a language becomes less common, cultural knowledge may be harder to pass on.

5. Which can help preserve language?

D дұрыс. Schools, families және media language preserve етуге көмектеседі.D правильно. Schools, families and media can help preserve language.

6. What can make a language feel alive and meaningful?

B дұрыс. Traditional songs, poems, films and books language alive and meaningful етеді.B правильно. Traditional songs, poems, films and books can make a language feel alive and meaningful.

7. What is the challenge mentioned in the final paragraph?

C дұрыс. Challenge — global languages үйрену, бірақ own language-ты respect and use ету.C правильно. The challenge is learning global languages while respecting and using one’s own language.

8. Which title best matches the passage?

A дұрыс. Best title language және cultural identity арасындағы байланысты толық қамтиды.A правильно. This title covers the main link between language and cultural identity.
